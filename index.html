Our story

We are
Sunshower Creatives.

A sunshower happens when the sun shines through the rain — two opposite forces creating something unexpectedly beautiful. That is exactly what we do.

Founded by Ankit Ghimire in Kathmandu, we believe the best brands live at the intersection of strategy and beauty. We don't separate creative from commerce — we make them inseparable.

We work with ambitious businesses — local, regional, and those building from Nepal outward — who want to be the brand people remember.
